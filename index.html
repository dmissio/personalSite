about me

I’m a front-end web developer with an eye for design.

I’ve been a full-time developer in Toronto since the start of 2016 bringing a varied skill-set and unique perspective to every project I undertake. I am a practicing writer, musician and artist and have been actively working in these fields for the past ten years which I believe has helped shape the way in which I approach web development. My experience as a chef, an educator and a communicator help to inform the way I unfailingly organize myself and take care to ensure that I provide a clear message across all devices that is as accessible as possible.
